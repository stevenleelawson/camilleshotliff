ORIGIN STORY

I was raised by a wild pack of coyotes in the high mountain deserts of Colorado. They taught me how to stay warm, forage for food, and keep undetected. These particular set of skills helped me in my teenage hobo career where I began to take notice of the artwork which adorned this countries freight trains and bridges. After spending many years absorbing America’s various forms of art, I found myself settled down as a river pirate in the same desert I prowled as a coyote pup. After long days of pillage and plunder, I would unwind by painting the treasures I captured, but after eleven years of this I decided I was sick of being sun burnt. So, I traded my first born for an art degree and landed myself an apprenticeship under a wise dragon slayer who lived in a castle atop a nearby mountain. In “The Dungeon” I learned and strengthened my tattooing skills, practicing on the prisoners and guards which live there. Armed with the knowledge I acquired, I made my way back to the heart of Denver where I have continued to strengthen my skills as an artist and human. With a BFA and seven years of tattooing under my belt, I eagerly await our consultation!
